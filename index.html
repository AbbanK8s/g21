FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does ceramic coating last?

Our premium 9H ceramic coating lasts 2-5 years depending on maintenance and driving conditions. We provide detailed care instructions and offer maintenance packages to extend longevity.

What's the difference between ceramic and graphene coating?

Graphene coating offers superior heat resistance, enhanced hydrophobic properties, and better flexibility compared to traditional ceramic. It's the latest advancement in paint protection technology.

Do you offer mobile detailing services?

Yes! We provide mobile detailing for select packages within Lahore. Contact us to check if your location is covered and to schedule an appointment.

How should I maintain my car after ceramic coating?

Wash regularly with pH-neutral shampoo, avoid automatic car washes, and use microfiber towels. We provide a complete maintenance guide and recommended products with every coating service.

Can you remove scratches from my car?

Our paint correction service can remove light to moderate scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation. Deep scratches that penetrate the clear coat may require touch-up paint or repainting.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and all major debit/credit cards. For premium packages, we also offer flexible installment plans.
